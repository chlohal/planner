Backup

Your schedule is automatically & anonomously backed up. You can opt out of this below.
Please note that your schedule will still be saved locally when this is unchecked.

Your notes (click in the center of a period box) are also backed up. Uncheck this box to opt out.

If you want your schedule to update on another computer or browser, you can copy the text from the box below, and paste it in the same place on another computer.


    You must exit the Configuration area to save your backup settings

Add Chromebook App

Do you want to have your schedule as an app on your Chromebook? Well, sadly, I can't do that (stupid security settings), but I can do the next best thing: a mini-app on your Chrome bookmark bar. Just drag the icon below to the bookmark bar!
To preview what the app does, click the icon once
